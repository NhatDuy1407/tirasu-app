Tirasu is a desktop tool for scheduling and uploading videos to multiple social media platforms from a single interface — with AI-assisted metadata generation.

TikTok YouTube Instagram Facebook

What it does

Everything in one place

Multi-Platform Upload

Upload the same video to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook in one workflow — no switching between apps.

Scheduled Publishing

Set a specific date and time for each platform. Posts go live automatically when scheduled, even while offline.

AI Metadata Generation

Powered by Google Gemini. Auto-generates platform-optimized titles, descriptions, and tags for each upload.

Local & Private

All data — tokens, metadata, history — is stored in a local SQLite database. Nothing leaves your device to any third-party server.

Per-Platform Controls

Configure audience settings, playlists, duet and remix toggles, custom captions — independently for each platform.

Upload History

Track every upload job — status, logs, errors, and timestamps — from a unified history view across all platforms.

Workflow

How it works

1

Connect Accounts

Authorize your social media accounts via their official OAuth flows. Tokens are stored locally only.

2

Pick a Video

Select a video file. Tirasu reads its technical metadata automatically — resolution, duration, codec.

3

Generate Metadata

Use the AI assistant to generate a title, description, and tags tailored to each target platform.

4

Schedule & Upload

Choose publish times per platform and let Tirasu handle the rest — immediately or at a set schedule.
